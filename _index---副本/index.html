About

Qian Yin is currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree at the National Engineering Research Center of Visual Technology (NERCVT), School of Computer Science, Peking University under the supervision of Prof. Siwei Ma (IEEE Fellow). She received her M.S. degree in Signal and Information Processing from the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC), where she was advised by Prof. Jianwen Chen. She was fortunate to collaborate closely with Prof. Xinfeng Zhang, Prof. Shanshe Wang, Dr. lili Zhao and Dr. Jiaqi Zhang. Her research interests include video and point cloud compression. She is also actively participating in the development of video/point cloud coding standards (AVS and MPEG).
